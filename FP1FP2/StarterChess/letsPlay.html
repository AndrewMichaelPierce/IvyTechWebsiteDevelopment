Let's Play!

There are many ways to play chess, probably the best for a beginner to to play with a friend or family member that is also interested. However there are also many online options and you can also play by e-mail.

Chess.com

Chess.com has been around for about fifteen years and has wonderful resources for new players! Beginning a game on this site is easy and there are a lot of tools to improve your chess.

lichess.org

lichess.org has been around for about five years but has quickly grown in stature in the chess community. With a slick interface and cash prize tournaments for titled players, lichess.org has been a destination for serious chess players for several years now.

United States Chess Federation

The USCF has been the United States' governing body for over-the-board chess tournaments since 1939. With their website you can find a tournament in your area and get started in tournaments at your playing level!
